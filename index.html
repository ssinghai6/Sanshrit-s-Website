Sanshrit Singhai

MS CSE, Machine Learning - Georgia Tech

About: Sanshrit Singhai is a graduate student at Georgia Tech . At GaTech he's actively involved in Machine Learning and is pursuing his MS in Computational Science and Engineering under the tutelage of Dr. Chloe Arson. He works at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning research as demanded by his focus on developing an AI toolbox for subsurface exploration that can be deployed through unmanned robots for potential extraterrestrial use cases.

Skills

Machine Learning, Aritificial Intelligence

I've worked on many projects that involves machine learning, and am currently a Graduate research assistant in DeepMelt Group. I have experience using Tensorflow, PyTorch, and Keras.

Python

I've had extensive experience with Python in the course of my research, classwork, and personal projects. Some of my personal projects in Python include generation of Random Field, Computational Geomechanics, neural model library, Pandas, NN trainer library, and many more.
